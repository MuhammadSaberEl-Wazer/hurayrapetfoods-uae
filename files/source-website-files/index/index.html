Yes, our food is suitable for cats aged four months and above. The balanced, high-protein formula supports healthy growth, energy, and immunity.

Yes, our packaging is fully recyclable and designed to minimise waste. We focus on ethical sourcing and responsible materials to protect pets and the planet.
